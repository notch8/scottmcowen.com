Scott McOwen 5-22-08

Been

  1. Born in Long Beach, California, raised from age of 7 in San Jacinto, CA on a small farm. Graduated from UCI, Seminary of the West, HBS-OPM program.
  1. Married and have raised two daughters and currently have four grandchildren from 1 to 7 years of age.
  1. Founded or bought 20+ businesses from exporting to Russia, Latvia, & Estonia to Bookshop-Café, Mobile Home Park, Shopping Center, Internet businesses of sheet-music, Natural Novelties, and Netroleum, but mainly retail and wholesale petroleum marketing, (Gasoline, Diesel and lubricants) in Southern California. The SOCO Group in Carlsbad, California is the company.
  1. Twenty years ago brought in professional management, went back to school and worked as a hospital chaplain, St. Joseph’s, Mission and Saddleback Hospitals.
  1. Have worked past 8 years as a chaplain to people in business that have lost a spouse to death, financial downturn or other major loss. I listen, understand and brainstorm. Examples: Three girls under 11 years of age in unrelated situations died the first weekend I was on call, time spent with a single mothers who’s young marine son was in a coma for 6 months, two men who died and they brought them back to life after some time, having someone tell their stories is healing, an older gentleman lost his wife to cancer and I was encouraging him to tell me some stories, when I ask for the most “fun time” he thought for what seemed to be 15 minutes, but it was a few long minutes and then he said, “I’m sorry, I can’t do that. I could practically read his mind and saw the most healing in a short period of time that I have ever seen with anyone. My strength as a chaplain is my weakness – I over identify therefore I become very close to the situation, but it is very hard on me. I can help people put money issues in perspective when dealing with so much death, even though many money situations can lead to bad health and even death through suicide and stress.
