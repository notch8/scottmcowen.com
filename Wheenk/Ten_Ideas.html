Ten (10) Entrepreneurial Ideas

  1. Listen
  1. Two (2)-life cycles – Products & Organizations – one curve.
  1. Three (3) Scenarios Bad – Expected (OK) and Good.
  1. Four (4) Financial Statements – Bank, Government taxes, Operational, Build Wealth (Long Term)
  1. Five Steps
    1. Business & Not for Profit Organizations – Risk & Reward
    2. Surround yourself with people of impeccable character
    3. Have a positive cash flow
    4. Satisfy customer’s & Members “perceived value”
    5. Minimize your risk & Maximize your rewards (play the game)
  1. Six ways to keep the organization healthy. Ask the following:
    1. What can the organization do without & when?
    2. What overall or category price increases can be made & when?
    3. Repeat question one now that the distance to the improvement goals are apparent.
    4. What individual product &/or service price increases are possible, over & above those in question?
    5. What can be done differently? Begin with the identification of product &/or services that are not contributing sufficiently to overhead & profit.
    6. Implement all these answers & changes as soon as possible & continue repeating the process by starting at (a.).
  1. Seven steps to do it:
    1. Plan
    2. Set Tasks
    3. Delegate
    4. Control & Monitor
    5. Listen & Learn
    6. Reward
    7. Repeat all the above – an on going process.
  1. Eight Disciplines to continue to learn about:
    1. General Management
    2. Human Aspects of Business and Organization
    3. Management Control
    4. Financial Management
    5. Marketing Strategy
    6. Decision Analysis
    7. Business, Government & the Global Economy & Society
    8. Creating & Managing Global Business & Organizational Relationships

9. Nine Uncontrollable Environmental Forces

1. -9. Changes in Macroeconomics, microeconomics, technology, governmental rules & regulations, physical environment, social forces, life styles, your mental ability & well being, your physical and personal health.

  1. Ten Steps to take entrepreneurial action

1. Listen & Learn 2. Focus 3. Plan-write it down (Business and personal life Plan) 4. Delegate 5. Control & Monitor (Systems & Processes) 6. Teach & Train 7. Learn & Listen 8. Reward 9. Improve (change & adjust) 10. Repeat all above steps.
